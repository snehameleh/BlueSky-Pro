Risk Architecture

Lose small. Win big.

BlueSky Pro

Every signal comes with a pre-calculated Stop Loss and two Take Profit levels — placed by the algorithm the moment the trade opens. No improvisation. No emotional "just a little more room" decisions.

Stop Loss

Losses end small.

A pre-calculated Stop Loss is placed the moment a signal fires — based on volatility and price structure, not arbitrary percentages. Losing trades typically close around -10% to -12%. No account-blowing drawdowns.

Example · $1,000 position

−$100 to −$120

Maximum loss · capped

Take Profit 1

Lock the partial.

When price reaches the first profit target, the system scales out 30% of the position — partial profit secured. The remaining 70% runs free until a confirmed trend exit. Even if the runner fully reverses, the locked partial cushions the damage — total damage shrinks by ~90%.

Example · $1,000 position
Even if runner reverses −$10

The 30% is locked. The rest depends on whether SL or TP2 is hit next — a $200-$320 gain if the runner makes it home.

Take Profit 2

Let winners run.

The second target captures the bulk of the move. Historical winners have exited between +15% and +38%, with a strong cluster around +25-30% on the runner portion.

Questions

Frequently asked.

How do I get access after purchasing? +

You'll receive an email immediately after payment. We add your TradingView username to the BlueSky Pro invite-only indicator within minutes, and you'll get the stock list and rulebook PDF right away.

Do I need trading experience? +

No. BlueSky Pro is designed for everyone from complete beginners to experienced traders. The rulebook walks you through every step — from placing your first order to managing a full swing portfolio.

Is BlueSky Pro automated? Does it place trades for me? +

BlueSky Pro is semi-automated. The indicator and alert system do the heavy lifting — identifying entries, exits, and stops. You place the actual orders manually with your broker, which keeps you compliant and in full control of your capital.

How many trades should I expect? +

BlueSky Pro is a swing trading system — the goal isn't frequency, it's quality. Typical trades last several weeks to a couple of months. You'll hold a diversified portfolio with new signals appearing as setups mature across the watchlist.

What if it doesn't work for me? +

Every plan comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you decide BlueSky Pro isn't the right fit, just email us within 30 days of purchase for a full refund — no questions asked.
